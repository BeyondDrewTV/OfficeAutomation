# Copperline — How to Get Your First Client This Week

## What this system does for you
Finds local businesses, scrapes their emails, drafts outreach, and sends it.
Your job: approve emails, answer replies, get on calls, close deals.

---

## Step 1 — Start the dashboard

Double-click `Launch Dashboard.bat`
Opens at http://localhost:5000

---

## Step 2 — Find leads and send emails (do this today)

1. Pick an industry: **plumbing**, **hvac**, or **electrical** to start
2. Type your city and state
3. Set limit to **20**
4. Click **⚡ Discover + Draft**
5. Click **📧 Find Emails** — scrapes websites for contact addresses
6. For any missing emails: Google the business name + city + "email" and paste it in manually
7. Read every email in the review panel — edit anything that sounds off
8. Click **✓ Approve** on the ones you're happy with
9. Click **▶ Send Approved**

That's it. You just sent cold outreach. Do this every week.

---

## Step 3 — When someone replies

They replied because they're curious. Your only goal: get on a call.

Reply fast (within the hour if possible). Say:

> "Great - I have 15 minutes Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon if either works?
> I can show you exactly what I'd set up for you."

That's all. Don't pitch in email. Get on the call.

---

## Step 4 — On the call

1. Ask: "What happens when a lead calls after hours and you're not there?"
2. Shut up and listen
3. Share your screen and open the demo:
   - `automation-agency-office/demos/missed_call_capture/index.html`
4. Walk them through it — let them type in the form
5. Say: "I can have this running for you in a week. Setup is $900, then $150 a month."
6. Send the proposal the same day (see `Copperline-Proposal-Template.md`)

**Close rate from cold email is roughly 1 in 5 calls.** Contact 20 businesses, get 3-5 replies, get on 2 calls, close 1. That's $900 + $150/month from 20 emails.

---

## Step 5 — They said yes. Now what?

1. Send invoice for 50% upfront ($450). Don't start until it's paid.
2. What you're actually building for them:
   - A missed call auto-text (use a service like **HighLevel**, **Twilio**, or **SimpleTexting**)
   - A web form that notifies them instantly
   - Test it with a real call before you hand it over
3. Deliver in 7 days
4. Ask for a testimonial and a referral

## Clients to avoid (from experience)

- Anyone who wants to "discuss equity" instead of paying
- Anyone asking you to build first and pay later
- Anyone who won't do a 15-minute call before buying
- Anyone who says $900 is too expensive (they'll be a nightmare client)
