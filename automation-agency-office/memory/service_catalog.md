## After-Hours Lead Capture

**What it is:**
A dedicated phone line for the business. When a customer calls and no one answers,
they hear a short message and immediately receive a text.
If they reply, the lead is logged and the owner is notified by text or email.
We set it up, we manage the infrastructure. The client does nothing technical.

**What it solves:**
Customers who call after hours and get voicemail almost never call back.
They call the next company on the list. This captures them before that happens.

**What's included:**
- Dedicated Twilio number provisioned by us
- Voice message played on missed call
- Auto-SMS sent to caller within seconds
- Inbound reply captured and logged to Google Sheets
- Owner notified by SMS and/or email on every lead
- Setup, testing, and go-live
- 30-day monitoring after launch

**Build time:** 3–5 hours per client

**Pricing:**
- Setup (one-time): **$750**
- Monthly (ongoing): **$150/month**
- Payment: 50% ($375) before work begins, balance at go-live
- Monthly billing starts after go-live

**Best for:**
Plumbers, HVAC, electricians, locksmiths, garage door repair, towing

**Not for:**
Restaurants, salons, retail, appointment-based businesses with front-desk coverage

## What the Client Provides

- Their preferred business phone number (we forward or replace as needed)
- How they want to be notified (text, email, or both)
- A short description for the voice message (or we write it)

That is all. No software to install. No accounts to create. No technical setup.

## Red Flags (Walk Away)

- Wants to own and manage the Twilio account themselves
- Already has a live answering service and is satisfied with it
- Chain or franchise location (can't make decisions)
- Wants equity or deferred payment
- Can't articulate what a missed call costs them
