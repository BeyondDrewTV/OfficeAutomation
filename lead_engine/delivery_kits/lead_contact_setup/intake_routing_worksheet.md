## 5. Scope Ceiling (Bounded Scope Enforcement)

This setup covers only the following. Anything not checked requires a change order.

- ☐ Phone / missed-call routing
- ☐ Website form / email routing
- ☐ Owner notification setup
- ☐ Follow-up timing configuration
- ☐ Basic lead logging (Google Sheet or equivalent)

**Explicitly out of scope:**
- CRM implementation or data migration
- Custom application development
- Multi-location or franchise routing
- Marketing, paid traffic, or lead generation
