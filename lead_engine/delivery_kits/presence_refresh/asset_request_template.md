# Presence Refresh — Asset Request Template
Version: v1 | Kit: presence_refresh_v1

## Purpose
Request all required assets from the client before starting work. Do not assume, invent, or source anything without owner-provided or owner-approved content. Missing required assets stop delivery.

**Rule: Work begins only after required assets are received.**

---

## Operator Instructions
Send the section below to the client (adapt to text, email, or in-person handoff). Fill in the receipt confirmation once assets arrive.

---

## Asset Request (Send to Client)

Hi [Owner name],

To complete your Presence Refresh, I need a few things from you. The more complete this is, the better the result.

### Required

**1. Business Information**

Please confirm or correct the following:

- Exact business name (as it should appear on all platforms):
- Primary phone number:
- Business address — or service area description if no storefront (e.g., "Serving the Dallas–Fort Worth area"):
- Business hours (include every day of the week, and note any closures or holiday hours):
- Website URL (if you have one):

**2. Services**

List your top 3–5 services in plain language. These go directly into your profile descriptions.

Example: "Exterior painting", "Interior painting", "Drywall repair", "Cabinet refinishing"

Your services:

**3. Service Area**

Which cities, neighborhoods, or counties do you serve? Even a rough area helps.

---

### Helpful (Send If Available)

**4. Photos**

Any of the following improve the end result significantly:
- Logo or wordmark (PNG, SVG, or JPG — higher resolution is better)
- Photos of your finished work (before/after pairs are ideal)
- Job-site or in-progress photos
- A photo of you or your crew (builds trust on profiles)

**5. Platform Links**

If you know your Google Business Profile listing URL or Facebook page URL, include them. Otherwise I'll locate them.

---

### Optional

**6. Style or Brand Reference**

Any color, style, or tone preferences I should follow when updating copy or selecting photos? (e.g., "Keep it simple and professional" or "We use navy blue and white.")
