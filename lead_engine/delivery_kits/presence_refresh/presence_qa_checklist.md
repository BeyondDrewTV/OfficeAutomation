# Presence Refresh — QA Checklist
Version: v1 | Kit: presence_refresh_v1

## Purpose
Run this after all cleanup work is complete, before handing off to the owner. This is the operator signoff check. Do not close out until every in-scope platform passes.

**Rule: Any item that fails blocks closeout. Resolve before issuing the handoff.**

---

## Google Business Profile QA

### Search Test
- ☐ Search the business name on Google — the profile appears in results
- ☐ Business name in the Knowledge Panel is correct
- ☐ Phone number in the Knowledge Panel is correct
- ☐ Hours show correctly for today's day of the week
- ☐ Address or service area displays accurately

### Profile Content
- ☐ Website link opens the correct page (no 404, no wrong page)
- ☐ Primary category is appropriate for the business type
- ☐ Business description is visible, accurate, and does not contain placeholder text
- ☐ Services list is visible and current

### Photos
- ☐ Cover photo is showing correctly
- ☐ At minimum 3 work/project photos are visible
- ☐ No obviously outdated or irrelevant photos remaining (that were in scope to remove)

---

## Facebook QA (If In Scope)

- ☐ Page name is correct
- ☐ Phone, address/area, and hours match GBP
- ☐ About section is accurate and current
- ☐ Cover photo and profile photo are current
- ☐ Website link is correct

## Cross-Platform Consistency QA

- ☐ Business name is the same on all updated platforms
- ☐ Phone number is the same on all updated platforms
- ☐ Hours are the same on all updated platforms
- ☐ Any platform showing stale info that was not in scope for this engagement: flagged in notes (not touched)

---

## Trust Copy QA

- ☐ No broken links in any description or about section
- ☐ No outdated service areas or discontinued services mentioned
- ☐ No placeholder text remaining in any field on any updated platform
