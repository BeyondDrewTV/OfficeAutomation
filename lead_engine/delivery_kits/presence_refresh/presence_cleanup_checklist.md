# Presence Refresh — Presence Cleanup Checklist
Version: v1 | Kit: presence_refresh_v1

## Purpose
Step-by-step work checklist for executing a Presence Refresh engagement. Complete every item in the active scope. Check off items only when done — not when started.

**Scope is bounded. Only work on platforms that are explicitly in scope for this engagement.**

---

## Pre-Work (Required Before Any Platform Edits)

- ☐ Asset request complete and required assets received (see `asset_request_template.md`)
- ☐ Access checklist confirmed — access to all in-scope platforms confirmed (see `access_checklist.md`)
- ☐ Before-snapshot taken — screenshots captured of the current profile state on each in-scope platform (see `closeout_snapshot_template.md` — Before section)

**Do not make any edits until all three pre-work items are checked.**

---

## Google Business Profile

### Basic Info
- ☐ Business name is correct and matches the owner's exact preferred name
- ☐ Primary phone number is correct and matches the owner's confirmed number
- ☐ Address is correct — or service area is accurately set (for service-area businesses with no storefront)
- ☐ Website URL is linked and opens the correct page
- ☐ Business hours are set for all seven days, including any closure or holiday notes
- ☐ Business description is updated — accurate, plain English, mentions primary services and area

### Services & Categories
- ☐ Primary category is set to the most accurate available option
- ☐ Services list is updated with the owner's confirmed service list
- ☐ At minimum 3 services are listed

### Photos
- ☐ Cover photo is current or updated (if new photo was provided)
- ☐ At minimum 3 work or project photos are present and current (upload new if owner provided them)
- ☐ Logo is uploaded (if owner provided a logo file)
- ☐ Outdated or irrelevant photos removed — only after owner confirmation, not unilaterally

### Trust Copy
- ☐ Business description reviewed for accuracy — no outdated service areas, wrong phone numbers, dead links, or stale copy
- ☐ Q&A section reviewed — misleading or outdated answers removed or corrected

---

## Facebook / Basic Social (If In Scope)

- ☐ Page name matches the confirmed business name
- ☐ Phone number matches GBP
- ☐ Hours match GBP
- ☐ Address or service area matches GBP
- ☐ About section updated to reflect current services and area in plain English
- ☐ Cover photo is current (update if new photo provided)
- ☐ Profile photo / logo is current
- ☐ Website link is correct

---

## Cross-Platform Consistency

Run this check after completing all in-scope platform updates:

- ☐ Business name is identical across all updated platforms
- ☐ Phone number is identical across all updated platforms
- ☐ Hours are consistent across all updated platforms
- ☐ Address or service area is consistent across all updated platforms
- ☐ Any inconsistency on an out-of-scope platform: flag it in notes, do not touch it

---

## Closeout Steps

- ☐ After-snapshot taken (see `closeout_snapshot_template.md` — After section)
- ☐ QA checklist completed (see `presence_qa_checklist.md`)
- ☐ Owner notified of what was changed
- ☐ Copperline status updated
