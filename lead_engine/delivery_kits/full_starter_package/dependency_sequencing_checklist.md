## Sequencing Decision

**Recommended sequencing: Presence → Lead & Contact Setup → Site**

Rationale: Presence and routing are lower risk and have fewer blocking dependencies. The site build comes last because the contact CTA must point to the routing destination that is confirmed and live from Lead & Contact Setup. Building the site before routing is confirmed risks having to update the CTA after launch.

## Contact Route Alignment (Pre-Build Gate)

Before starting the site build, confirm:

- ☐ Lead & Contact Setup routing destination is confirmed and on file
- ☐ Website CTA phone/email matches the routing destination
- ☐ This alignment is documented in the master intake worksheet

Do not begin the site build until this is confirmed. Misalignment discovered after launch requires a site update and re-test — avoidable with this gate.
