# Follow-Up & Reminder Setup — Message Template Pack
Version: v1 | Kit: follow_up_reminder_setup_v1

## How to Use This Pack
These are the starter message templates for the owner's follow-up and reminder flow. The owner reviews and approves every template before any message is sent. No template goes live without the approval checkbox checked.

Fill in each [placeholder] with real business details during setup. Owner reviews the final draft — not the placeholder version.

**Note:** These templates use the owner's name and business identity. Drew sets them up, but the owner is always the sender identity. No message goes out under Drew's name or from Drew's number.
