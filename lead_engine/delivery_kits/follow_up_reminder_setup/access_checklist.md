## Policy

**Send-from identity:** The owner is always the sender identity. No reminder goes out from Drew's number or email. Drew sets up the flow, but every message arrives from the owner's business contact.

**No automation tool:** If no automation platform is available or in scope, setup defaults to a manual reminder reference card. The owner keeps the card and uses it to send follow-ups by hand. Manual is always a valid option.

**Access:** Drew does not retain login credentials after setup. Delegated access or a shared login is confirmed with the owner and revoked at the owner's request.
