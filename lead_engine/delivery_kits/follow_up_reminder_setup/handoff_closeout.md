## How to Pause or Stop a Reminder (Safe-Pause Procedure)

If the owner needs to stop a reminder from going out — a problem with a client, a change in workflow, or a system issue:

**For manual-send triggers:** Simply do not send the message for that contact. No system change is needed.

**For automated triggers:**
1. Contact Drew immediately — do not wait.
2. Drew will pause the trigger within [agreed response time, e.g., same business day].
3. Until Drew confirms the pause, manually intercept any outgoing reminders if possible by logging into [platform] and disabling the trigger.
4. Drew will log the pause and confirm in writing.

## Revision Boundary

**What is in scope without a change order:**
- Updating copy in an existing approved template (e.g., adjusting wording, updating a phone number)
- Adjusting timing on a single trigger (e.g., change day 3 to day 5)
- Adding or removing a placeholder field from an approved template

**What requires a change order:**
- Adding a new trigger not covered in the original cadence worksheet
- Adding a new send channel (e.g., adding email when only SMS was set up)
- Integrating with a new tool or platform
- Building automation where only manual send was originally configured
- Any inbound reply handling or conversation routing

Contact Drew to discuss before starting any out-of-scope change.
