### Estimate for: Mike Harrington
**Date:** April 9, 2025
**Estimate #:** EST-2025-031
**Valid for:** 7 days from the date above (expires April 16, 2025)

---

### What This Estimate Covers

Replace the kitchen faucet and supply lines at 412 Birchwood Lane.

**Scope summary:**
- Remove and dispose of existing kitchen faucet
- Supply and install new Moen Arbor single-handle pull-down faucet (brushed nickel)
- Replace both hot and cold supply lines under the sink
- Inspect shut-off valves and replace if corroded
- Test all connections and confirm no leaks before leaving

---

### Investment

**Total: $385**

---

### What Is Included

- New faucet and supply lines (materials included)
- Labor for full installation
- Inspection of shut-off valves
- Leak test and confirmation
- Old fixture and packaging hauled away

---

### What Is Not Included

- Any plumbing work discovered behind the wall or under the floor
- Cabinet repairs or countertop modifications
- Any scope not listed above

Any additional work will be discussed and approved as a change order before we proceed.
